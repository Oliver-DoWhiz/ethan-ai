Product thesis

Ethan’s real moat is a disciplined template engine.

The first version is deliberately narrow: a real-estate video production workflow that can ingest raw footage, organize the cut, and output platform-ready campaign assets with premium taste.

Why now

  • Real-estate teams already generate raw footage faster than they can edit it.
  • Campaign packaging matters as much as the timeline itself.
  • Template discipline beats fake “infinite creativity” for repeatable ops.
  • A local-first Rust MVP makes the system easy to review and evolve.

System

Ingest, understand, plan, review, export.

Ingest

Footage counts, listing photos, floorplan coverage, voice notes, and neighborhood b-roll become the operator brief.

Plan

Ethan creates the middle layer Logan asked for: a reusable edit plan instead of a one-off output blob.

Package

The system generates titles, description, thumbnail copy, chapters, subtitles, and delivery targets alongside the cut.
