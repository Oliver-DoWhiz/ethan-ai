# Ethan AI Market Notes

## Product framing input

The shared ChatGPT conversation clarified the right first wedge:

- Ethan is not a generic “video editing chatbot.”
- Ethan should act like a **template-driven video production engine**.
- The first use case is **open-house walkthrough video production**.
- The real middle layer is the **edit plan**.

## Implications for the MVP

1. The product should output more than a timeline.
2. Multiple platform formats must feel related, not improvised.
3. Premium brand presentation matters because the target inventory is high-end.
4. The first version should look operationally credible even before full rendering exists.

## Template set chosen for v1

- Open House Masterpiece
- Hero Short
- Listing Explainer

This is intentionally small enough to preserve quality and coherence.
